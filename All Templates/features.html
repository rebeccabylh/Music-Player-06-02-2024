Why a music player

You may be asking yourself "Becca why are you doing a music play promotion, that's so silly-" SHHHHH FREE WILL
Anyway, I chose a music player promotion website because one I'm NOT original :/, second I can't do much with the limited brain usage I have okay? My creative vision for this assigment was just make sure that majority of everything was pink and has the best songs ever, you can fight me on this I WILL win.

I had a very good idea for what I wanted the website to look like but what happens with every project I do, I can't do exactly what I want so I settle for what I can do. I wanted a pink background, flowers in the corners of the website, different text font, what I should right for both the developer and this page, and what pictures I wanted to have. But with my current skills, I think I'm fine with what I have right now as it is still very pretty to me, so I'll settle for it.
The songs I have chosen are all from the songs I have in my playlist on spotify because that's where I listen to my music :>
